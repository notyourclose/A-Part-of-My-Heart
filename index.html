A Part of My Heart
Is Still Written for You.

Some words are hard to say out loud. So I turned them into a page you can read slowly, like a late night song.

I didn’t make this to make you feel guilty. I made this because my heart still had things it needed to say.

Read my story

What I Never Said Properly

About four months ago, we started something between us. Maybe it wasn’t perfect… maybe you didn’t feel it the same way I did. But for me, it was real from the beginning.

I was scared of losing you. That’s why I held on so tightly. I tried everything I could to keep us together… even when things were slowly falling apart.

I spoke to my family, you spoke to yours… we tried to make things right in every possible way. But somehow, it was never enough.

The Things I Did

I know I made mistakes. I tried too hard sometimes. I held on too much.

There were moments I acted out of pain… not because I wanted to hurt you, but because I felt like I was losing you.

Even then, I never wanted revenge, never wanted to break you… I just wanted to feel like you cared about me.

What Hurts The Most

What hurts the most is not just the distance… it’s how slowly you started to let me go.

The way you stopped talking, the way I became less important… like I was already gone from your world.

Even today, you couldn’t give me even a little time… but still, I’m here loving you the same way.

The Truth In My Heart

I won’t lie… without you, everything feels empty.

But I also know I can’t force you to stay. I can’t make you feel something you don’t feel anymore.

So this is not me asking you to come back… this is me just being honest about my heart.

One Last Thing

I loved you in a way that was real to me. Not perfect… not always right… but real.

Even if you walk away, a part of me will always care for you.

Take care of yourself… even if it’s not with me anymore.

What I Truly Want

I don’t want to keep going in circles like this anymore... hurting each other, coming back, and breaking again.

I just want peace. I want a life where I can breathe without feeling this weight every day.

If that peace is with you, I would still choose it without hesitation. But if it’s not... then I have to learn how to find that peace within myself.

Because living like this, repeating the same pain again and again... is something my heart can’t carry forever.

Made with love and pain.

I Love You Forever And Ever and Ever !
